# Task 4 - IoT Case Study

## Topic
Future of IoT in Smart Cities

## Description
This case study explains the future scope and importance of Internet of Things (IoT) technology in smart cities. It covers the role of IoT in transportation, healthcare, energy management, security systems, and environmental monitoring.

## Concepts Covered
- Smart City Infrastructure
- Smart Traffic Management
- Smart Street Lighting
- Smart Waste Management
- Smart Water Systems
- Smart Healthcare
- Energy Management
- Artificial Intelligence Integration
- 5G Communication

## Technologies Used
- IoT Sensors
- Cloud Computing
- Artificial Intelligence
- Big Data Analytics
- Edge Computing
- Wireless Communication

## Benefits
- Improved public safety
- Better transportation systems
- Energy efficiency
- Environmental sustainability
- Smart resource management

## Challenges
- Security and privacy risks
- High implementation cost
- Network dependency
- Data management issues
